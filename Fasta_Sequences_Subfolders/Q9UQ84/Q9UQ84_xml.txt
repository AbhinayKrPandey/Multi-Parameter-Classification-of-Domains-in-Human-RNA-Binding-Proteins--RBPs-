  <comment type="function">
    <text evidence="6 7 12 13 14 16 18 19 21 24 25 31">5'-&gt;3' double-stranded DNA exonuclease which may also possess a cryptic 3'-&gt;5' double-stranded DNA exonuclease activity. Functions in DNA mismatch repair (MMR) to excise mismatch-containing DNA tracts directed by strand breaks located either 5' or 3' to the mismatch. Also exhibits endonuclease activity against 5'-overhanging flap structures similar to those generated by displacement synthesis when DNA polymerase encounters the 5'-end of a downstream Okazaki fragment. Required for somatic hypermutation (SHM) and class switch recombination (CSR) of immunoglobulin genes. Essential for male and female meiosis.</text>
  </comment>
