  <comment type="function">
    <text evidence="7">Plays a role in nonsense-mediated mRNA decay. Does not have RNase activity by itself. Promotes dephosphorylation of UPF1. Together with SMG7 is thought to provide a link to the mRNA degradation machinery involving exonucleolytic pathways, and to serve as an adapter for UPF1 to protein phosphatase 2A (PP2A), thereby triggering UPF1 dephosphorylation. Necessary for TERT activity.</text>
  </comment>
  <comment type="subunit">
    <text evidence="4 5">Interacts with TERT, PPP2CA and SMG1. Part of a complex that contains SMG1, SMG5, SMG7, PPP2CA, a short isoform of UPF3A (isoform UPF3AS, but not isoform UPF3AL) and phosphorylated UPF1. Not detected in complexes that contain unphosphorylated UPF1.</text>
  </comment>
