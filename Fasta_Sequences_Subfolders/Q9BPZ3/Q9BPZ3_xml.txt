  <comment type="function">
    <text evidence="2">Acts as a repressor in the regulation of translation initiation of poly(A)-containing mRNAs. Its inhibitory activity on translation is mediated via its action on PABPC1. Displaces the interaction of PABPC1 with poly(A) RNA and competes with PAIP1 for binding to PABPC1. Its association with PABPC1 results in disruption of the cytoplasmic poly(A) RNP structure organization.</text>
  </comment>

    <text evidence="2 3">Interacts with the second and third RRM domains and C-terminus regions of PABPC1 in a 2:1 stoichiometry.</text>
